3. SUMMARY OF THE GAME CONCEPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Star Wings is an infinite 2D side-scrolling action game where the player 
controls a space explorer moving through a hazardous environment. The objective 
is to survive as long as possible by navigating vertically to avoid obstacles, 
shooting down hostile enemies, and collecting health packs to stay alive. The 
game's difficulty scales dynamically, with increasing player speed and shifting 
spawn rates, providing a progressively challenging experience.


4. DESCRIPTION OF CORE FEATURES IMPLEMENTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Health System: Implemented in 'PlayerController.cs', managing energy levels with 
  a dynamic UI slider using a color gradient (Green to Red). Damage is taken from 
  obstacles and enemies.
- Scoring System: Score increments when passing through specific zones or 
  destroying enemies, displayed via TextMeshPro on the UI.
- Health Packs: Randomly spawned collectables that restore health upon contact, 
  ensuring survival during longer runs.
- Projectile Attacks: Left Mouse Click triggers a shooting mechanism, allowing the 
  player to defend against incoming enemies.
- Dynamic Enemy AI: Enemies spawn at random intervals and heights, requiring the 
  player to either dodge or engage them with projectiles.
- Speed Progression: The player's forward velocity increases over time, ramping 
  up the intensity of the gameplay.

6. CONTROL GUIDE (KEYBOARD/MOUSE INPUT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Vertical Movement: W/S or UP/DOWN Arrow Keys
- Shoot Projectiles: Left Mouse Button (Mouse 0)
- Restart Game: Navigation buttons on the Game Over screen
- Exit: Navigation button on the Main Menu


7. DESCRIPTION OF THE CREATIVE FEATURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Dynamic Difficulty & Weight-Based Spawning: The 'ObjectSpawner' uses a weighted 
  algorithm that evolves over time. As the game progresses, obstacles and enemies 
  become more frequent, while health packs become rarer, creating a natural and 
  engaging difficulty curve.
- Visual Polish (Camera Shake & Sprite Feedback): Hits with obstacles trigger 
  a screen shake effect for impact, and the player sprite changes dynamically 
  to reflect vertical movement (Look Up/Down/Neutral).
